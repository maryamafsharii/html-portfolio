The Best Movies According to Maryam

My top 3 movies of all-time.

little women

Little Women is a beautiful and inspiring film that shows the strength, dreams, and love between women. The story feels timeless and full of emotion.

Emily in Paris

Emily in Paris has a charming and colorful atmosphere. The show beautifully captures the elegance of Paris and makes you want to go there.

Garfield

Garfield is such a funny and lovable character. His lazy attitude and sarcastic humor make the movie really entertaining.
